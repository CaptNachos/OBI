CRIME PREVENTION ON THE INTERNET FOR THE BETTER AND HEALTHIER USER EXPERIENCE

by: OBI Visual Editor and Executive Officer

The internet that we face today is not the same as it was before. Communities nowadays are become toxic, scammers showing showing up in online shopping; and even scammers in online games. this is why this community was created; to prevent innocnet people from being scammed, bullied by toxic users from the internet. Our community is open to all internet users who want to join us fight crimes and prevent evil actions in order to keep the peace online.

 "In all my years of gaming and using the internet, i have interacted with lost of people online and already have a lot of experiences dealing with them. Most people only communicate seldomly, ol when needed. Some people trashtalk and spread toxicity which is not helping at all. Only a few people nowadays are friendly and non-toxic which are fun to play and talk with. I have also been scammed of some in-game items which I worked hard for. We must remember that online or not, thievery and scamming are the same and must be punished for their crimes scammers commit."

- OBI Visual Editor, 2018

"As an internet user I have came across different kinds of people while on the internet be it on gaming or a disscussion. In the gaming community there are people who are fun to be with and play with. Then there are the toxic people in the community who constantly blame other people for their own mistakes and curses them even there is no need to. When you play with these kind of people they will set the mood of the game weather you will enjoy it or not. There is also those who will try to scam you, either ny hacking your account or stealing your money or ingame items. These are the types of people who makes the internet a dangerous place. Which is why the Online Bureau of Investigation was created"

 -OBI Executive Officer
